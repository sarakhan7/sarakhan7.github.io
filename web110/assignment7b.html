Through this assignment, I learned how databases connect information across multiple tables using keys. The CustomerID links customers to their orders, while ProductID connects items to order details. This relational system prevents duplicate data and makes updates easier. I now understand why primary keys must be unique and how foreign keys create relationships. These concepts will help me design better websites that store user data, product catalogs, or order histories efficiently. For example, when I build an online store, I'll structure tables similarly to Northwind to manage customers, products, and orders properly. The most valuable lesson was seeing how complex queries can pull specific data from multiple connected tables.
