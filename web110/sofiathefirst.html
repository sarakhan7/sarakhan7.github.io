Sofia the First is about a young girl named Sofia who becomes a princess after her mother marries King Roland II of Enchancia. The series follows Sofia as she navigates her new royal life, attends Royal Prep, and learns what it means to be a princess, often with the help of her magical amulet. The amulet allows her to communicate with animals and grants her special powers based on her good deeds.
